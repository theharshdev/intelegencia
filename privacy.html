Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and physical address when you interact with our Services, register for an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact our support team. Additionally, we may automatically collect non-personal data including browser type, IP address, and interaction metrics to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized to provide, maintain, and improve our services globally. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • Send administrative information, such as service announcements and policy updates.
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance website and product functionality.
  • Enforce our terms of service and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Intelegencia does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information globally within our corporate group, or with trusted service providers who adhere to strict data security protocols to assist us in operating our services. We may also disclose data if legally required to mitigate imminent harm or comply with judicial proceedings.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking frameworks to monitor site activity, store your preferences, and track the performance of marketing campaigns. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or notify you when a cookie is being sent. However, some portions of our services may become inaccessible without cookies.

5. Security of Your Data

We employ industry-standard, robust encryption and security procedures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@intelegencia.com.
